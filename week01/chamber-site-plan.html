Guarapiche Chamber of Commerce

Named after the Guarapiche River that flows through the city of Maturín

About the Site

Purpose

The Guarapiche Chamber of Commerce aims to help the city of Maturín thrive and grow by strengthening the bonds of its community. Our desire is to impulse the business owners and entrepreneurs that reside within our borders to in turn make Maturín strong and better every day.

Scenarios

  • How can I as a business owner become part of the Guarapiche Chamber of Commerce?
  • Where can I learn more about the history of this organization?
  • Who are the leaders of the chamber?
